Task 5 — Planck PR4 Low-l TT Comparison
========================================

Result: PR4 = PR3 for low-l TT. Identical chi2.

Tristram et al. (2024, A&A): "The only part that still relies on PR3
is the low-l temperature likelihood, Commander, as we do not anticipate
significant improvements at large scales in temperature between PR3 and PR4."

PR4 improvements are:
  - High-l: HiLLiPoP replaces plik (l > 30)
  - Polarization: LoLLiPoP replaces simall (low-l EE/BB)
  - Low-l TT: UNCHANGED (same Commander data)

Therefore:
  chi2_DGF  (l=2-30) = 66.49  (same as PR3)
  chi2_LCDM (l=2-30) = 55.998 (same as PR3)
  Delta_chi2 = -10.49 (LCDM favoured, same as PR3)
  DGF sign pattern: unchanged

The H0 shift (PR4: 67.49 vs PR3: 67.36) comes entirely from
the high-l likelihood change, not from low-l data.
